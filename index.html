The Hollow

How To Play

  1. Scan the Prologue card to begin the story.
  2. Scan Fragment cards to hear audio clues.
  3. Match fragments to the correct Chapter.
  4. Scan Chapter cards (hard cardboard cards).
  5. Complete all chapters and scan the Epilogue.

Game Order:
Prologue → Fragments → Chapters → Epilogue
