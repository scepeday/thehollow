The Hollow

How To Play

  1. Scan the Prologue card to open the story.
  2. Use the Instructions button on the Prologue screen if you need help.
  3. Scan Fragment cards to hear audio clues.
  4. Scan Chapter cards to hear chapter memories.
  5. Arrange the cards on the Solve screen to unlock the ending.

Game Order:
Prologue → Fragments → Chapters → Solve → Ending

A young girl stands alone in a dark forest at night. A soft beam of light shines down on her from above, making her the focal point of the image.

Surrounding her in the darkness are small glowing lights, representing fairies. The lights appear delicate and magical, almost like sparkling fireflies, but they are scattered in a way that feels unsettling rather than comforting.

The forest around her should be shadowy and slightly obscured, with hints of trees and branches fading into darkness.

The overall mood should feel both magical and eerie, the glowing lights are beautiful, but the scene as a whole feels tense and unsafe, as if something is watching her.
